Best smartphone 2020: Latest phones with prices in Kenya

a month ago 33524 views by  Simon Ayub

It is now more than a decade since Apple revolutionized the market with the unveiling of its first smartphone. It did not take long for other firms to mimic the technology and produce various smartphone models for different consumer markets. The overflow of such devices makes it even harder to realize the best smartphone in Kenya today. What features should you look for and does the price matter when buying a smartphone? Popular brands boast of the best technology and knowing how peculiar Kenyans are; you will find many with Samsung, Nokia, and Huawei brands.

Image: pexels.com
Source: UGC

Consumers perceive brands based on their reputation. Moreover, in the communication industry, one's popularity is as high as their last product. Therefore, if a predecessor of a smartphone line were not as great as expected, buyers would find it hard to trust the ensuing products. Such is the reluctant attitude of most buyers that lead them to dig deeper into the terminologies of these smartphones.

So, what features would determine the best phones in Kenya? Does the design matter? Are the internal components the most important? Decisions! Decisions! It never gets old. Ultimately, you ought to leave the store with a gadget that you consider worthy of your hard-earned cash.

The ideal budget smartphones in 2020

The good news is, with so many phones on the market, you can get a suitable device without breaking the bank. Unfortunately, choosing the best budget smartphones in Kenya that will offer you excellent service for a long time becomes much harder with a vast pool to pick from. Hopefully, this list of the best phones in Kenya and their prices will come in handy.

1. Infinix S5

Image: infinixmobility.com
Source: UGC

“Brilliant experience starts from here,” that is according to Infinix's slogan for the release of this smartphone. The phone's design does not disappoint. If you are looking for something sleek and still affordable, then this ought to be on your shopping list. It is a great successor of the Infinix S4, which launched earlier.

The display, performance, camera and battery life are outstanding. You can easily mistake this gadget for a high-end one.

Specifications

  • 6.6 inch IPS LCD with a screen resolution of 1600 x 720 pixels
  • Design dimensions are 164 x 76 x 7.99mm
  • Android 9 Pie
  • 6 GB RAM, 128GB ROM and Micro SD up to 128GB
  • 16 MP wide-angle back camera and 32 MP secondary camera
  • Battery 4000mAh

You can get your hands on the Infinix S5 if you are ready to part with KES: 15, 990.

2. Tecno Spark 4

Image: aboukam.net
Source: UGC

If you are a techie, looking for cheap smartphones in Kenya; however, would like something that can perform exceptionally, this is the ideal one. Tecno Spark 4 is an upgrade of its predecessor, the Tecno Spark 3. The main highlight of this amazing gadget is the camera, which is feature-rich.

The phone has a premium look, something that is mostly absent from many budget phones. It almost lacks bezels except for the camera notch on top and a hardly noticeable lower edge border. Other than this, everything else is excellent.

Specifications

  • 6.52 inch IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen with a screen resolution of 1600 x 720 pixels
  • Design dimensions are 166.7 x 75.8 x 8.4 mm
  • Android 9 Pie
  • 3 GB RAM, 32GB ROM, and expandable Micro SD
  • 8MP main camera and 8 MP secondary camera
  • Non-removable 4000mAh battery

You can get this phone online or from different offline outlets at KES.9,549.

3. Tecno Camon 12

Image:tecno-mobile.com
Source: UGC

Tecno Camon 12 is another one of the best cheap phones in Kenya in 2020. This fantastic model was launched in November, and it came with the latest technology highlights at the time. What makes the Tecno Camon 12 amazing are the Dot-in-display and Triple AI camera features.

The phone's design is impressive as it features a 90% screen ratio. This means that you can have some immersive screen time. Interestingly, the front-facing camera is “inside” the display; a first for Tecno smartphones.

Specifications

  • 6.55 inch IPS LCD with a resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels
  • Design dimensions are 164.2 x 76.1 x 8.15 mm
  • HiOS based on Android 9 Pie
  • 3 GB RAM, 32GB ROM and expandable Micro SD up to 128 GB
  • 2.0 GHz Octa-core processor
  • 16MP main camera and 8 MP secondary camera
  • Non-removable 4000mAh battery

The recommended retail price for the Tecno Camon 12 is KES.12, 999 in Kenya. You can get it at a lower price if you know where to look; however, expect to spend in that range.

4. Samsung Galaxy A20

Image: zoomit.ir
Source: UGC

Samsung Galaxy A20 was released on April. Samsung is currently the most prominent phone seller in the globe. Therefore, they have different models for every consumer in the market. The A-series, mostly the Galaxy A20, is one of the best cheap phones in Kenya.

It features a massive 6.4-inch screen with 720 x 1560P Infinity display to offer you the best viewing experience. It also comes with a high-performance octa-core CPU, which is combined with 2 GB RAM to guarantee you a fast, reliable, and smooth user experience.

Specifications

  • 6.4 inch Super AMOLED capacitive touchscreen, 16M colours
  • Corning Gorilla Glass 3
  • Design dimensions are 158.4 x 74.7 x 7.8 mm
  • Android 9 Pie, One UI
  • Exynos chipset
  • 3 GB RAM, 32GB ROM, and expandable Micro SD
  • 2.1 GHz Octa-core processor
  • 13MP/5MP ultra-wide main camera and 8MP secondary camera
  • Non-removable 4000mAh battery
  • Supports fast battery charging at 15W

The Samsung Galaxy A20 is available at approximately KES 17,000.

Image: infinixmobility.com
Source: UGC

  • 6.5 inch IPS LCD screen with 1600 x 720 pixels
  • Design dimensions are 165 x 76.3 x 8.7 mm

Source: UGC

Specifications

  • Design dimensions are 158.9 x 76.9 x 8.1 mm
  • Android 8.1, EMUI 8.2
  • 3GB RAM, 32GB ROM or 4GB, 64GB RAM and expandable Micro SD
  • 1.8 GHz Octa-core processor
  • 13MP/2MP dual main camera and 16MP secondary camera with HDR
  • Non-removable 4000mAh battery

Image: Jumia.co.ke
Source: UGC

The Huawei P20 Lite is one of the top phablets in Kenya, with a large 5.84-inch screen. It comes with an impressive 2280 x 1080 pixels' display, which delivers bright, vivid images for the best viewing experience. The phone runs on Android 8.0 operating system and is powered by Kirin 659 chipset with eight cores.

Specifications

  • Octa-core (4 x 2.36 GHz & 4 x 1.7 GHz Cortex A53) CPU
  • 4GB RAM and 64 GB memory, expandable up to 256 GB
  • 16.0 MP + 2 MP dual rear camera and 24 MP front camera
  • Dual-SIM support with 2G, 3G, and 4G LTE technology
  • Android 8.0 operating system
  • 3,000 mAh battery
  • Fingerprint sensor unlock

Huawei P20 Lite is available at approximately KES 23,000.

Specifications

  • 3GB RAM and 32GB of internal storage

Source: UGC
